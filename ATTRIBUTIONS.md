## Images

All image assets under `web/assets/` are **original works generated in-repo** by
project scripts — no third-party or Kairosoft assets are used. They are
Kairosoft-*inspired* (style only) and released under **CC0 1.0 Universal**; they
may be replaced by commissioned/CC0 art later via the same `web/assets/manifest.json`.

Current building/sprite set (Kairosoft visual upgrade, Sprint 2) is generated by
`scripts/gen_building_sprites.py` to the Building Sprite Spec in
`docs/design-language.md`. (The earlier retro set from `scripts/generate_iso_assets.py`
has been superseded.)
