Background & Objective

Kairosoft games shower the player with little payoffs. CarbonSim only emitted abstract particle bursts. Sprint 4's objective: add spatial floating feedback tied to real events, then finish the roadmap's perf/a11y/cross-screen items.

Inputs & Scope

  • Plan: plans/2026-06-14-kairosoft-feedback-juice-plan.md; roadmap index plans/2026-06-14-kairosoft-visual-roadmap-index.md.

  • Out of scope: engine/snapshot changes; commissioned art; rounded-pixel font.

Assumptions & Constraints

  • Feedback is cosmetic over events already surfaced (cash/compliance/XP); no new engine state.
  • Respect reduced motion and the 30fps throttle / caps.
  • Toast = textual; floaters/emotes = spatial (no double-signaling).

Methodology

Phase-by-Phase Analysis

Phase 01 — Floating feedback layer

Isocity.float(text,color) + Isocity.emote(symbol): rising/fading on-canvas pop-ups anchored above the player plot, capped, no-op under reduced motion.

Phase 02 — Event wiring

game.html fires 💚/−t/−cost on abatement, 🪙/+t on offsets, 😊/😡 on compliance flips, ⭐/"LEVEL UP!" on level-up.

Phase 03 — A11y, cross-screen, perf

Measured WCAG ratios; introduced --accent-ink for small accent text (5.4:1) and darkened green-badge ink (5.3:1); wired feedback into co-op + summary; confirmed reduced motion; bounded perf with caps.

Findings & Recommendation

  • Juice landed: real Activate clicks fire emote+coins (≈3,300 px change); floaters animate and fade.
  • Accessibility verified + fixed: all key pairs now meet WCAG 4.5/3.0; 2 prior failures corrected.
  • Consistent across screens: game, co-op, and summary all show the full Kairosoft stack.
  • Recommendation: roadmap is done — ship it. Optional polish in the backlog below.
