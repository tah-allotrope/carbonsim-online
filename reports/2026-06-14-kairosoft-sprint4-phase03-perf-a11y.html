4Math / Algorithm Used

WCAG 2.1 relative-luminance contrast: L = 0.2126R+0.7152G+0.0722B (linearized), ratio = (L₁+0.05)/(L₂+0.05). Searched darker shades until small accent text and green-badge ink cleared 4.5:1.
