Sprint 1 — Asset Pipeline

  • Created a clean web/assets/ structure with fonts, sprites, tiles, audio, and manifest metadata.
  • Added the FastAPI /assets static mount and an AssetLoader preloader in web/js/assets.js.
  • Authored ATTRIBUTIONS.md and procedural placeholder sprites to avoid unreliable third-party downloads.

Sprint 2 — Design Tokens

  • Replaced neon palette with parchment/teal retro tokens and beveled chrome shadows.
  • Integrated Press Start 2P as the display font while keeping sans/mono for body and stats.
  • Retuned animations and documented the design language in docs/design-language.md.

Sprint 3 — Isometric Renderer

  • Built web/js/isocity.js with a 2:1 isometric projection and deterministic company-to-plot mapping.
  • Capped individual plots at 16 and aggregated overflow into district tiles.
  • Implemented a reduced-motion static render path and made isocity the default renderer in all modes.

Sprint 4 — Full Reskin

  • Audited all four HTML screens for neon remnants and hardcoded colors.
  • Retokenized inline styles, Chart.js colors, tables, modals, and the year-transition overlay.
  • Removed web/js/skyline.js and its references.

Sprint 5 — SFX, Perf & A11y

  • Re-voiced web/js/audio.js to square/triangle chiptune cues with short envelopes and arpeggios.
  • Converted web/js/effects.js and canvas particles to blocky pixel-style bursts.
  • Hardened isocity.js: DPR cap of 2×, adaptive particle budget, debounced resize, background-tab guard.
  • Added global :focus-visible, fully wired prefers-reduced-motion, and fixed contrast across all screens.
  • Updated docs/design-language.md with verified accessibility results.

Verification & Quality

  • Full backend/engine test suite passes on every phase: 92 passed.
  • Manual frontend audits completed for palette contrast, focus visibility, reduced-motion behavior, and visual consistency.
  • No new build steps or frontend frameworks introduced; the stack remains vanilla JS + canvas + FastAPI.

Recommended Next Steps

  • Play-test the full three-year single-player and multiplayer flows with sound and animations enabled.
  • Run Lighthouse/axe on each screen in a real browser to capture automated accessibility scores.
  • Profile isocity.js on low-end Android and iOS devices to validate the 30fps floor under real conditions.
  • Consider V2 features (shock events, bot liquidity, tutorial mode) only after the core game loop is proven end-to-end.
