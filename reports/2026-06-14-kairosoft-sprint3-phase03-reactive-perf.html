3Logic Flow

4Math / Algorithm Used

vitality = (compliance_gap > 0 ? 0.6 : 1) × (emissions > 200 ? 0.6 : emissions > 120 ? 0.8 : 1); desired = max(2, round(min(plots×2, 14) × vitality)). Count changes are applied as a delta so existing citizens keep position.
