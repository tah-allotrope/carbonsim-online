06 / Limitations / Second-Best Alternative

Limitations / Second-Best Alternative

  • Dynamic dispatch blind spot: from engine import * and card-effect dispatch via string matching may hide indirect callers. Mitigation: included string/route-name searches in grep.
  • 2 "known failures" already fixed: The plan cited 2 failing tests, but all 83 tests pass. They were likely resolved during Sprint 2's layout migration. No additional test-fixing needed in PHASE-02.
  • Second-best alternative: Use a coverage tool (pytest-cov) to automatically identify dead code. More accurate but requires setup time; manual grep was faster for this codebase size.
