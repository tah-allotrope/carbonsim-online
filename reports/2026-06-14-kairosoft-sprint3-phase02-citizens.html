1Phase Header

Populate the city with wandering chibi citizens using the Phase-1 animation core, correctly layered with buildings.

2Input → Output Summary

InputOutput
Animation core + citizen sheet; no citizens placed A citizen pool (sized to plots, capped 14) that wanders the city, flips by direction, and is depth-sorted with buildings by screen-y

3Logic Flow

cityBounds() from plot positions→ spawnCitizens (stable across refresh)→ updateCitizens (seek target, re-target, flip)→ draw: merge buildings+citizens, sort by y

4Math / Algorithm Used

Wander = seek a random target within the city bounding box; on arrival (dist<2) pick a new target. Move by a normalized vector × per-citizen speed; flip = dx < 0. Depth = single painter pass over {buildings (base screen-y), citizens (y)} sorted ascending.
